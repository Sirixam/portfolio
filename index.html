How I Work

01
Discover

I start with the player. What emotions should they feel? What fantasy does the game fulfill? Research, competitive analysis, and player psychology set the foundation.

02
Design

Core loops, mechanics, and systems mapped before a single line of code. GDDs, flowcharts, and diagrams that keep the whole team aligned.

03
Prototype

Build it fast, break it faster. Rough prototypes answer critical design questions early — when iteration is cheap and pivots are painless.

04
Iterate

Playtesting, metrics, and feedback loops drive refinement. I follow the data without losing sight of feel — because great games are both measured and felt.
